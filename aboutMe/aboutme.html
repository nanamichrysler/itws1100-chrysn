ABOUT ME

I am a sophomore at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, dualing in Cognitive Science and ITWS with a minor in Marketing.

A little more about me, I am from Davis, CA. I chose to attend RPI not only because of its academic rigor and excellence in blending STEM and humanities, but because my grandfather is also an alumn!

Flying across the country and moving to the opposite coast for college was an exciting new chapter. On campus, I am involved in a few clubs, including the Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers, Consulting Club, and Marketing Club. I am also a Division 3 athlete and part of the Women's Swimming Team!

In this advanced digital age, I am passionate about leveraging my understanding of the human brain & behavior and my technical knowledge to create solutions to marketing & consulting cases.

Thank you for stopping by!
