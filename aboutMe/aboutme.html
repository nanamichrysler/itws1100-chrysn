ABOUT ME

I am a sophomore at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, dualing in Cognitive Science and ITWS with a minor in Marketing.

I'm originally from Davis, CA, and came to RPI for its strong blend of academic rigor and interdisciplinary learning. I also have a personal connection to RPI, as my grandfather is an alum, which makes this school especially meaningful to me.

On campus and outside academics, I stay active in a variety of organizations. I am a swimmer on the Varsity Women's Swim & Dive Team and I am extremely grateful to pursue my athletic goals at the collegiate level. I am also an I-PERSIST Calculus mentor assisting incoming freshmen transition to college life while supporting their Calculus studies. Furthermore, I am a member of the Rensselaer Consulting Club, the Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers, ACM-W, Women in Busines, and I am a sister of Alpha Phi! Through these organizations, I have developed great leadership and collaboration skills.

I am passionate about understanding how people think and behave, and using that insight to create data-driven solutions in marketing, consulting, finance, and user experience. My goal is to work at the intersection of human behavior and technology, and am always looking for opportunities to something to apply my skills and learn from new experiences.
